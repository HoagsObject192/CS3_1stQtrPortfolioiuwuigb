## Details!
    - As the website aims to have a Wikipedia-like format, it now includes a user forum - Tusok for Talk.
      > The different branches and sub-branches of STEM, humanities and other interesting topics will be
        featured in 'Kalyes' or roads.
      > We also include a 'Sulok of the Month' which aims to bring various different suloks or corners of
        different topics that can pique interest which may even help others find their desired career path.
    - Usage of User Info:
      > Users may opt to remain anonymous or to sign up for an account where they can log in and at some point,
        get a biweekly newsletter about new features and different featured Kalyes.
      > Users can also upload pdf files, articles, or cool websites that they think others may find interesting.
      > In the STEM, HUMMS, INFO, AND MORE webpages, those small little user inputted tidbits of information appears
        and get cycled through many others where older ones get archived.
      > Also, at the top of the different webpages, we include 'Merienda ng Bayan' which is a compilation
        of the tidbits of info that create a cohesive and comprehensive idea of the main concept.
